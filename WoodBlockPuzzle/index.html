Retro 14 Block Puzzle Classic - Mobile Puzzle Game

Retro 14 Block Puzzle Classic is a simple block puzzle with a warm wooden style. It's light, calm, and easy to enjoy, whether you've got a few minutes or a whole afternoon. The game features 14 different block shapes and a classic 10×20 board for endless puzzle fun.

Game Features

  • Small & Smooth - Only around 10MB and runs steady at 30fps
  • Cozy Look & Feel - Soft wooden blocks and natural colors
  • Simple Fun - Classic 10×20 board and easy controls
  • A Gentle Challenge - 14 block shapes for varied gameplay
  • No rush, no stress - friendly puzzle experience
  • Available on iOS and Android devices

How to Play

Drag and drop wooden blocks onto the 10×20 board to create complete lines. When you fill a complete horizontal line, it disappears and you earn points. With 14 different block shapes, every game offers new challenges and keeps your mind engaged.
