Sky Run – Geometry Jump - Mobile Platformer Game

Sky Run – Geometry Jump is an exciting mobile platformer game where players help Claire rescue the prince from the Moving Land. The game features twisting platforms, spinning obstacles, and challenging gameplay that tests your reflexes and timing.

Game Features

  • Simple tap controls - tap to jump, hold to jump farther
  • Dynamic moving platforms that twist, spin, and shift
  • Multiple playable characters including Claire, Rowena, Isabeau, Arwen, Coraline, Selene, Lyra, Zinnia, and Aveline
  • Challenging levels with increasing difficulty
  • Beautiful graphics and smooth animations
  • Available on iOS and Android devices

How to Play

Master the art of jumping in Sky Run – Geometry Jump. Time your jumps perfectly to navigate through twisting platforms and avoid falling. Each character has unique abilities that add variety to the gameplay experience.
